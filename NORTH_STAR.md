# Carta’s north star

## The singular promise

**Know the cup you love—whether you made it or found it.**

Carta is a personal coffee memory built around one unit: the cup. A cup has two
equally important contexts:

- **Home** — prepared deliberately, measured, adjusted, and made again.
- **Café** — prepared by someone else, received, experienced, and returned to.

This is not a brewing tool with a café feature, or a café passport with recipes
attached. It is one record of taste across two ways of encountering coffee.

## The gap

Coffee products usually choose a side.

Brewing apps compete on recipes, timers, equipment, scale integrations, and
increasingly complete logs. Café products compete on discovery, place ratings,
check-ins, maps, and social proof. Both preserve activity. Neither gives a person
a coherent memory of their own palate across home and away.

The open question is more human: *What do I love in a cup, and does that preference
travel between what I make and what I am served?*

Carta can answer without pretending correlation is causation. It records both
contexts in the same language—Coffee, Preparation, Cup—and lets the pattern emerge.

## The loop

### Home

1. Choose the coffee in hand.
2. Begin from the last cup.
3. Change one variable.
4. Record the preparation and experience.
5. Return to the best known cup.

### Café

1. Name the place.
2. Record what was served.
3. Mark the experience in the same 1–9 language.
4. Leave one honest line.
5. Return to the place or coffee worth finding again.

### Together

Carta shows one preference in two rooms. It does not turn home and café into a
contest. It makes them legible beside one another.

## Product restraint

A feature belongs in the primary experience only if it answers one of four questions:

- What cup is in front of me?
- How was it prepared?
- What did I experience?
- Is it worth making or finding again?

Everything else moves behind the record, into settings, or out of the product.

### Keep prominent

- Equal Home and Café entry points
- A shared Cup language: hedonic 1–9, descriptors, one line
- Last and best home cups
- Last café cup and places worth returning to
- The quiet comparison between home and away

### Demote

- Setup administration, preferences, users, sync, import, and the manual
- Detailed origin traceability, photography, address lookup, and spend
- Charts that do not change the next decision

### Preserve without promoting

- Existing records and full export
- Offline ownership and open schema
- Advanced details for people who have them

Restraint must never destroy the record.

## North-star measure

**Return rate:** the share of recorded cups that lead to a repeated home baseline,
a return café visit, or another encounter with the same coffee.

Supporting measures:

- Median time to record a Cup
- Share of records with only the honest minimum
- Home-to-café and café-to-home coffee matches
- Active coffees or cafés with a clear “best known” cup

Total fields completed, time in app, and feature usage are not success measures.

## Experience principles

- **Yin and yang, not primary and secondary.** Home and Café carry equal visual weight.
- **Cup is the invariant.** Preparation changes; experience is comparable.
- **Continuation over capture.** Restore context before asking for input.
- **Honest sparsity.** Missing data is better than invented precision.
- **Warm instrument, not dashboard.** Editorial type, tactile paper, one red signal.
- **Progress without gamification.** No streaks, badges, feeds, or completion pressure.
- **The ritual stays primary.** Carta serves the coffee, never the reverse.

## Refinement sequence

1. Reduce primary navigation to Home, Café, and Record.
2. Give Home and Café matching editorial structures: context, last cup, one primary
   action, and a short path back to what mattered.
3. Make Record the meeting place—one Cup stream, balanced context summaries, and
   only the progression that helps a future decision.
4. Move Coffees, Setups, preferences, sync, import, and administration behind
   secondary actions.
5. Remove café spend and activity-count summaries from the primary surface.
6. Next, add one explicit “make again” / “return here” signal and measure return rate
   locally without adding a social or recommendation layer.

## The sentence to protect

**One cup. Two contexts. Your taste is the through-line.**
