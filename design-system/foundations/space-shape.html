Space, shape & glyphs

Square by default, flat, hairline rules, no shadows — inherited from Quiet Matter. Two sanctioned exceptions: 2px chip rounding, and a small set of typographic glyphs.

Shape
--radius: 0
--chipr: 2px

--radius is 0 everywhere. --chipr (2px) is the one sanctioned rounding — chips and inline code only.
