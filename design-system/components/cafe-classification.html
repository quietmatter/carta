Café classification

The reach — how much of a coffee's road, farm to cup, reads from the customer's side of the counter. A description of the café, never a score; badges stay monochrome, carried by type — never the ember, never a fill.

The four depths
○ Countergood coffee, made fast and made right. Nobody narrates.
◎ Housethe bag is named, the menu has intent. Ask, and they answer.
◉ Roasterythe roast in the room; the grind adjusted before your cup.
● Originthe farm in the cup: producer, lot, process, freely told.

No depth outranks another — a lens in Find, never a sort. Keepers attest facts (the bag named, what the counter showed); the reading follows: the deepest fact standing.
